TAXES IN THE INFORMAL SECTOR

These taxes affect operations of businesses in the informal sector;

VEHICLE INCOME TAX (VIT)

  • This is a tax collected from commercial vehicle operators on quarterly basis.
  • It is obligatory for all commercial vehicle operators to buy VIT stickers from any Domestic Tax Revenue Office on quarterly basis
  • These stickers are in various categories and rates depending on the vehicle’s passenger capacity and type of operation e.g. taxis, trotros, tour buses, tipper trucks, etc.
  • The sticker must be pasted on the front windscreen
  • VIT monitoring is done with the assistance of the Ghana Police Service

TAX TO BE PAID BY PERSON WHO OWNS A COMMERCIAL VEHICLE AS PROVIDED IN REGULATION 22
